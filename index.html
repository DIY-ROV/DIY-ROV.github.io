Welcome to Our Project

We are a team working on a DIY underwater ROV for exploration and learning. Explore the sections to learn more about our project!

Power Supply

The ROV is powered by a rechargeable battery pack that supplies energy to the motors and control systems. We use a **12V lithium-ion battery** to ensure stable and long-lasting operation.

Wiring & Electronics

The wiring setup includes **motor controllers**, a **waterproof casing** for electronics, and a **tethered communication system** to send signals between the ROV and the operator.

Final Product

Once assembled, the ROV undergoes **testing in water** to evaluate stability, maneuverability, and video transmission capabilities. The final design ensures smooth underwater exploration.

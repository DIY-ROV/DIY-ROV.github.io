Welcome to Our Project

We are a team working on a DIY underwater ROV for exploration and learning. Our project focuses on creating an affordable, remotely operated vehicle (ROV) capable of underwater navigation.

DIY Underwater ROV

The DIY underwater ROV is designed to be an affordable and functional exploration device. It incorporates waterproof motors, a lightweight frame, and a camera system for real-time feedback.

Challenges

Building an ROV comes with challenges such as waterproofing, designing an efficient propulsion system, and ensuring smooth control mechanisms.

Estimated Cost

The estimated cost for building the ROV ranges between $200 and $500, depending on materials and components used.

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to design a functional, low-cost underwater ROV that can explore and capture video footage in water environments.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes and connectors
  • Waterproof motors
  • Battery and power distribution system
  • Camera for underwater recording
  • Control system (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or custom-built)

ROV Design

The design of the ROV is focused on being compact, lightweight, and modular. The ROV features a frame built from PVC pipes, making it both sturdy and buoyant. It includes space for a camera system, motors for propulsion, and a control system that can be customized for different purposes.

Power Supply

The ROV is powered using a rechargeable battery pack that supplies energy to the motors and control systems. The power system is designed to be efficient and easy to replace, ensuring the ROV has long-lasting energy during missions.

Wiring & Electronics

The wiring setup includes motor controllers, a waterproof casing for electronic components, and a tethered connection for communication. Proper wiring is essential to ensure the ROV operates without any power loss or electrical malfunction.

Final Product

After extensive testing, our ROV is fully operational. It maneuvers effectively underwater and provides real-time video feedback, offering a comprehensive solution for underwater exploration.

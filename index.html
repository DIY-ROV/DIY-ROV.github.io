DIY Underwater ROV

This project involves building a remotely operated vehicle (ROV) for underwater exploration.

Challenges

Challenges include waterproofing, designing an efficient propulsion system, and ensuring smooth control mechanisms.

Estimated Cost

The estimated cost ranges between $200 and $500, depending on materials and components.

Project Goal

Our goal is to design a functional, low-cost underwater ROV that can explore and capture video footage in water environments.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes and connectors
  • Waterproof motors
  • Battery and power distribution system
  • Camera for underwater recording
  • Control system (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or custom-built)

ROV Design

The design includes a frame made from PVC pipes, motorized thrusters, and a camera.

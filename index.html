Welcome to Our Project

We are a team building a DIY underwater ROV for exploration, research, and learning.

DIY Underwater ROV

Our ROV is a remotely operated vehicle designed to explore underwater environments while being controlled from the surface.

Challenges

Challenges include waterproofing components, designing an efficient propulsion system, and ensuring smooth controls.

Estimated Cost

The estimated budget for the ROV ranges between $200 to $500, depending on material choices and upgrades.

Project Goal

Our goal is to create a low-cost, efficient ROV for underwater exploration, education, and environmental research.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes and connectors
  • Waterproof motors
  • Battery and power distribution system
  • Underwater camera
  • Control system (Arduino/Raspberry Pi)

ROV Design

The ROV frame is made from PVC pipes, powered by thrusters, and includes a waterproof camera for visibility.

Power Supply

We use a **12V lithium-ion battery** to power the motors and electronics, ensuring stable performance underwater.

Wiring & Electronics

The ROV is wired with **motor controllers, a waterproof casing, and a tethered connection** for communication.

Final Product

After assembly, the ROV is tested in water to ensure proper movement, camera function, and communication.

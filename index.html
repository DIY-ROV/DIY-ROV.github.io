Welcome to Our Project

We are a team working on a DIY underwater ROV for exploration and learning. Our project focuses on creating an affordable, remotely operated vehicle (ROV) capable of underwater navigation.

ROV Design

The ROV design focuses on simplicity and efficiency. The frame is made from lightweight PVC pipes, ensuring buoyancy and durability.

Challenges Faced

We encountered issues with waterproofing and motor efficiency, requiring adjustments to improve performance.

Project Cost

The total cost was approximately $500, covering materials, motors, and electronics.

Project Goal

Our goal was to create a fully operational underwater ROV capable of capturing footage and collecting data.

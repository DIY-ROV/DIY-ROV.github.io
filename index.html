Welcome to Our Project

We are a team working on a DIY underwater ROV for exploration and learning.

DIY Underwater ROV

Our ROV is a custom-built submersible designed for underwater exploration.

Challenges

Building an ROV comes with challenges such as waterproofing, propulsion, and control.

Estimated Cost

The estimated cost for building the ROV is $200-$500.

Project Goal

Our goal is to design a low-cost, functional underwater ROV.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes
  • Waterproof motors
  • Camera system

ROV Design

The ROV consists of a PVC frame, motorized thrusters, and a control system.

Power Supply

The ROV is powered by a rechargeable battery.

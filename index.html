Our DIY ROV

Welcome to Our DIY Underwater ROV Project!

We built a low-cost, functional underwater ROV for exploration and research. Designed for under $375, it features a wireless remote, a waterproof casing, and a camera for capturing underwater footage.

Key Features:

  • Affordable & Accessible – A budget-friendly alternative to commercial ROVs.
  • Custom Engineering – Efficient motor control, buoyancy adjustments, and durable waterproofing.
  • Easy to Operate – Wireless remote control for smooth navigation.
  • Built on Open-Source Platforms – Making adjustments easy.

Challenges

We faced multiple challenges including waterproofing, motor efficiency, and buoyancy control. Overcoming these required testing and innovation.

Cost

Our ROV was built for under $375, significantly cheaper than commercial alternatives while maintaining quality and functionality.

Goal

Our goal is to make underwater exploration accessible and affordable while promoting STEM education and engineering creativity.

Materials

We used PVC pipes, waterproof motors, a camera module, and 3D-printed components to construct our ROV.

Design

Our design focuses on a compact, modular frame with efficient motor placement for stable maneuverability.

Power

The ROV is powered by a 12V battery, ensuring long operational times with efficient energy consumption.

Wiring

Our wiring system ensures reliable communication between the controller and motors while remaining waterproof.

Current Prototype

Our latest prototype is fully functional, featuring improved stability, better waterproofing, and a clear camera feed.

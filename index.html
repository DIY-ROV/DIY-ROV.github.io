DIY Underwater ROV

Welcome to the DIY Underwater ROV project! This project involves building a remotely operated vehicle (ROV) for underwater exploration.

Challenges

Creating an underwater ROV comes with challenges such as waterproofing, designing an efficient propulsion system, and ensuring smooth control mechanisms.

Estimated Cost

The estimated cost for building the ROV ranges between $200 and $500, depending on the materials and components used.

Project Goal

The goal of this project is to design a functional, low-cost underwater ROV that can explore and capture video footage in water environments.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes and connectors
  • Waterproof motors
  • Battery and power distribution system
  • Camera for underwater recording
  • Control system (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or custom-built)

ROV Design

The design includes a frame made from PVC pipes, motorized thrusters for movement, and a camera for underwater exploration.

Power Supply

The ROV is powered using a rechargeable battery pack that supplies energy to the motors and control systems.

Wiring & Electronics

The wiring setup includes motor controllers, a waterproof casing for electronic components, and a tethered connection for communication.

Final Product

Once assembled, the ROV is tested in water to evaluate its stability, maneuverability, and video transmission capabilities.

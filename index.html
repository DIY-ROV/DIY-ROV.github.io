Welcome to Our Project

We are a team working on a DIY underwater ROV for exploration and learning.

DIY Underwater ROV

The DIY underwater ROV is designed for affordable underwater exploration.

Challenges

Waterproofing, propulsion, and control are major ROV challenges.

Estimated Cost

Between $200 and $500, depending on components.

Project Goal

Our goal is to build a low-cost, functional ROV.

Materials Needed

  • PVC pipes and connectors
  • Waterproof motors
  • Battery and control system
